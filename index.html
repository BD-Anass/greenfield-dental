Our Philosophy

Why Greenfield Actively Supports Your Dental Tourism

We didn't build a clinic in Vietnam to take advantage of patients looking for cheaper options. We built it because we saw how badly dental tourism could go wrong — and decided to create a better alternative built on international standards, real accountability, and genuine care.

Built for International Patients — Any Country, Any Language

Greenfield Dental™ was created for patients worldwide who deserve world-class dental care without paying world-class prices. No matter where you're from, our team communicates in your language and delivers care to the highest international standards.

We're the Clinic — Not a Booking Agent

We're not a middleman connecting you to unknown practitioners. Greenfield Dental™ is the practice. Our internationally trained director personally oversees every clinical protocol, every team member, and every patient outcome — full accountability from day one.

Save 50–70% Compared to Home Country Prices

Whether you're from Europe, the Middle East, Asia, or Oceania — the savings are dramatic and real. Premium dental treatment in Vietnam costs a fraction of what patients pay at home, with zero compromise on materials, technology, or clinical quality.

We Stay With You — Long After You're Home

Most overseas clinics go silent the moment you board your return flight. Greenfield doesn't. Every patient has access to follow-up support, remote consultations, and professional guidance — regardless of which country they flew in from.

Why Most Dental Tourism Goes Wrong

Many clinics around the world attract patients with low prices — but cheap rarely means safe. Here's what patients from every country discover too late:

  • No way to verify who is actually performing your treatment
  • Sterilisation and safety protocols that don't meet international standards
  • Language barriers leading to miscommunication and wrong outcomes
  • Zero aftercare support once you're back home
  • Quotes that change mid-treatment — with no recourse

These aren't just inconveniences — they can mean failed treatments, repeated procedures, and thousands spent fixing someone else's mistakes.
